Amity International Model United Nations

Amity International Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nations spanning over three days. At AMIMUN, delegates gain in-depth knowledge of diplomacy, international relations, and the inner workings of the United Nations. Currently, in it's Ninth Edition, AMIMUN has set a certain standard for the conduct of MUNs throughout the Nation with its innovative themes, focus on pertinent issues and stellar level of debate. Through debating issues that confront world leaders and drafting resolutions, one can enhance their critical thinking, public speaking and leadership skills. Moreover, the combination of the depth of research required and the understanding one must-have of others' views and attitudes, due to the interactions with multitudes of other young minds from various backgrounds, give the students a deep insight into the real-world problems. They then become aware of the causes of conflict between nations which leads them to a better understanding of the interests and motivations of others. Thus, AMIMUN follows through with the Preamble to the Charter of the United Nations: " To practise tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbours " .

International Platform

Amity is made up of a multitude of people, from various backgrounds, interact and work with one another. AMIMUN takes advantage of this, allowing for a huge international platform, where ideas can be propagated, opinions formed, and views widened. Past editions of AMIMUN have been conducted in association with international bodies like the United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan (UNIC), Medecins Sans Frontieres, and Amnesty International India, and included delegates and dignitaries from Europe. AMIMUN truly is the best place to not only to showcase one's talent but further grow as a delegate and an individual.
