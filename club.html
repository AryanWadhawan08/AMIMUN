Message from Patron-in-Chief

Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan

Founder President, Ritnand Balved Education Foundation (RBEF), Chairman, AKC Group of Companies

It is a matter of great pride that Amity University Uttar Pradesh (AUUP) is organising the 10th edition of Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN’21), first time on the virtual platform, on the theme “Winds of Change: Navigating Through A New Normal in The Multipolar World” from Saturday, the 20th March, to Sunday, the 21st of March 2021. The event becomes all the more important in the backdrop of COVID-19 pandemic and has given us a unique opportunity to witness indomitable human spirit not only to withstand but also thrive in the face of any unprecedented challenge. With our belief in harnessing excellence in the field of innovative and adaptive education, whilst keeping the holistic approach intact, Amity strives to make AMIMUN’21 an experience that will uphold the mission of encouraging students to pursue futuristic frontiers ideas to change the world for better. Amity aims to consolidate AMIMUN’s worldwide recognition and its status as one of the top MUNs across the continent with the participation of delegates from across the world. ... I am confident that this event will provide a platform to young people to come forward, redefine their “new normal” and provide their ideas, solutions, and suggestions to build a better future and will connect brilliant minds to deliberate on real issues and suggest solutions for that. The teaching philosophy of Amity particularly focuses on values and Sanskars and importantly, the understanding of close cross-cultural nuances. At Amity, the students are nurtured with such attributes in an environment that motivates analytical thinking on matters that concerns the humankind. AMIMUN caters the mission of Amity and further complements it by providing a representational prevue of United Nations functions and its deliberation on crucial global issues. AMIMUN'21 will provide an opportunity to the students to become familiar with global concerns and the working of the United Nations where the delegates will attempt to resolve regional and international problems in a powerful, yet engaging manner through debates, diplomacy and negotiations. It will prove to be a platform to commence an optimistic outcome for the world andthe youth. As the leaders and changemakers of tomorrow unite at this conference, they will hone their skills of leadership, teamwork, expository and persuasive writing, debating and negotiations, and learn the significance of such traits to become successful professionals in future. All participants, from India as well as from diverse regions of the globe such as USA, Canada, Romania, UAE among others, will find AMIMUN'21 to be a convention where their unique talents and capabilities will enable them to achieve professional growth. I convey my appreciation to Dr. Alpana Kakkar, Dy. Dean (Students Welfare), Dr. Nitasha Hasteer, Dy. Director (Academics) and all the hardworking team members of AMIMUN’21 including Mr. Meshuwa Mawthoh, Secretary General, Mr. Rohan Nagpal, Director General, Ms. Simran Malhotra, Charge D’ Affairs, Ms. Hiba Mukhtar Dedmari, Chef de Cabinet as well as members of Organizing Committee and other brilliant students. I also compliment Dr. Balvinder Shukla, Vice Chancellor, AUUP, for her valuable support and guidance. The most strategic and visionary leadership of Dr. Atul Chauhan, Chancellor, AUUP and President, Ritnand Balved Education Foundation (RBEF), would lead to outcome based and result oriented success of AMIMUN '21. I welcome all the participants and wish them a pleasant experience with debating which will go a long way in their personal and professional growth. I am confident that they will cherish this enriching and engaging environment of the Amity Universe on virtual platform as they participate in AMIMUN’21. I wish AMIMUN’21 a great success.

Dr. Atul Chauhan

Chancellor, Amity University, President, RBEF and Patron-in-Chief

It gives me immense pleasure that Amity University Uttar Pradesh is organizing the first e-edition of Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN’21) on the 20th & 21st of March, 2021 and will host brilliant minds from leading Universities of India and the world. With the aim of promoting a spirit of discourse as a means of raising & resolving current issues while adapting to the new normal that the world has entered into, AMIMUN’21 this year is themed on “Winds of Change: Navigating through a new normal in the Multipolar World”. It is a conference that will provide a learning environment, the benefits of which can be significant in the journey of participating students who aspire to become future leaders.... This year’s edition of AMIMUN’21 Conference will be the first e-AMIMUN edition and will host over 300 delegates from across the globe. The theme perfectly incapsulates the challenging times during the pandemic and the relentless determination of each individual to succeed. Due to the recent global pandemic the World has witnessed a breakthrough and organizations across the globe has reformed their existing global policies and procedures. AMIMUN'21 will be a platform for the delegates to learn, understand the diplomatic relations between Nations, inculcate leadership skills and acquire intellectual competence to be socially responsible. It is my firm belief that by encouraging students to organize and participate in such events, we push the boundaries of creativity and innovation, and help them in thinking out of the box. It not only prepares the future leaders but initiates and sparks the feeling of empathy and social responsibility amongst the future generation.  I welcome the distinguished guests at AMIMUN’21 on the virtual platform and am certain that they will serve as role models for the students participating in the Conference. The vast experience and inspiration expressed in their words will help the delegates realize the significance of skills such as strategic thinking and critical analysis as well as the importance of positivity, humility, and compassion in their journey to make a real difference in the world.   AMIMUN’21 promises to be a conference of high repute owing to the sincere efforts of the devoted organizing members and the team of committed students. I extend my best wishes for AMIMUN’21 to be a reverberating success and hope that it builds upon the foundation laid by its outstanding previous editions.

Message from Patron

Prof. (Dr.) Balvinder Shukla

Vice Chancellor, AUUP and Patron

I am pleased to share that Amity University Uttar Pradesh is organising first online edition of Amity International Model United Nations Conference (AMIMUN) on the theme ‘Winds of Change: Navigating through A New Normal in the Multipolar World”, from March 20 - 21, 2021. The current worldwide scenario due to COVID-19 pandemic has caused huge reforms in the policies and procedures of organisations globally. Amity has also brough tremendous shift in the functioning of the university and taken several initiatives with the support of latest technology to ensure uninterrupted services to all stakeholders, continued learning, education, research, etc., AMIMUN’21 is one of such initiatives which is being organised virtually to energize connection among students from across the globe and give them the chance to partake in conciliatory conversations on convincing issues. ... A Model United Nations is a scholarly simulation of the different procedures of the United Nations, with understudies addressing different nations. Not only has the fame of MUNs taken off, it has also settled as a unique stage where the promising delegates come together and learn myriad skills. AMIMUN has helped members from across the world to observe the extraordinary relevance of learning foreign relations and understanding the working and meaning of the United Nations. Constructing harmonious relations, security, social advancement, better expectations of the world and common liberties, are various viewpoints that are underscored by the young minds at AMIMUN. The theme for AMIMUN'21 “Winds of Change: Navigating through A New Normal in the Multipolar World”, has been meticulously chosen to make sure that the young assume the responsibility to rise from the myriad of issues and find a solution. AMIMUN’21 aim to present a worldwide viewpoint through a space that will mix countries to meet and make a reality where we discover development and tranquility in concurrence. This virtual MUN will be a one-of-a-kind experience, with varied participants from universities from all around the world, especially when the physical borders are replaced by connections of internet and accessibility. I take this opportunity to convey my best wishes to the delegates and wish them a great learning experience. I am certain that the hard work and commitment of the organizing committee members and the Secretariat will make AMIMUN’21 a splendid success! Best Wishes,
