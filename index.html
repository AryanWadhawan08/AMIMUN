Message from Secretary General

Dear Delegate,
The world went through an unprecedented time in the history of the human race. Yet, in the midst of uncertainty, I hope that winds of change sweep away with it the misery.  Thus, to remain actuated and go into the correct direction, I invite you to the first edition of online Amity International Model United Nations, (AMIMUN '21) on Saturday, March 20th to Sunday, March 21st ,2021.
By following the legacy and success of previous editions, AMIMUN has set a bar within the Indian Mun circuit with an International base of participants. The secretariat of AMIMUN’2021 hopes to follow the same path and bring you an unparalleled debating experience while adapting to the current world order.
Amity International Model United Nations Conference 2021 is a platform for a boundless learning experience and a place to voice your suppositions, opinions while learning the skills of diplomacy. Through this conference, we wish that you experience the most captivating moments whilst sensitizing yourself with the dynamic changes that the world is going through today and encourage the highest calibre of discussion. We look forward to the fervour and enthusiasm which the delegates will bring with them thereby giving an encompassing comprehensive experience and highlighting issues to deliberate upon for a better future of humankind.
I would also like to take this opportunity to thank our Founder President Sir, our Faculty Coordinators, our Partners and Sponsors for their unremitting support for E-AMIMUN '21, and making this conference possible. On behalf of the secretariat, I extend a warm invitation to all delegates and hope this conference will be as productive for you, both as an individual and as a delegate. For any queries or concerns kindly feel free to reach out to us, we would be glad to assist you.


Sincerely,
Meshuwa K. Mawthoh
Secretary-General
Amity International Model United Nations 2021
