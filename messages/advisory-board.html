Message from Advisory Board

Mr. Siddhant Treasure

Secretary General, AMIMUN '20

“I believe in change. I believe in the power it has to unite us and ignite us.” - Uzo Aduba
Model United Nations conferences are not only verbal debates but a clash of core ideas and their application to the real world. This aspect of MUNs to focus on actionable change is something I believe is especially important as it lays a clear focus on problem-solving instead of abstract, principled debate. This focus on problem-solving is what sets MUNs apart from other activities since they actively incentivize the creation of a wide knowledge base built on strong research and develop clarity of action. problems. The Amity International Model United Nations Conference has become a stalwart in the circuit as an incredible stage for the young to see how the world functions and manages its issues while guaranteeing phenomenal debate and extraordinary levels of quality and hospitality. During my tenure as the Secretary General of the 9th edition of this conference, I learned essential values like responsibility, discipline, and most importantly leadership and teamwork through empathy and humility. The mammoth task of organizing a flawless conference is incomplete without the backing of an ever passionate and driven team, a team that soon became family. This year as a part of the Advisory board for AMIMUN’21, I am certain that this edition will continue to build on this tremendous legacy and become a fine illustration of excellent debate and discourse. I am sure that my juniors have corroborated an incredible experience for all the participants. It is indeed my honor and privilege to welcome you all to the e-Edition of Amity International Model United Nations Conference, 2021.
“Remember, each one of us has the power to change the world.” – Yoko Ono All the very best!

Aakarsh Shrivastava

Dept. Secretary General, AMIMUN '20

Amity International Model United Nations has always believed that activities like MUNs bring about tangible change, and help shape the leaders of the future. The team behind this conference believes that the power of student discourse can bring out the solutions to the problems which plague the world today.
I truly feel honored to be part of the advisory board of one of the biggest MUNs in the country, with students of all domains, spending their time working on problems that are necessary to be acted upon. Rich with experience and led by some of the best debaters in the circuit, the conference strives to bring out the best in each individual associated with it and promotes the values of the United Nations whilst working towards a better future.
This year the AMIMUN team is bringing the online edition of AMIMUN conference which strengthens my belief that even a pandemic can't stop any of us to collaborate, discuss and debate to bring about a change in society. It is just a matter of finding a way to bring about that change and that is exactly what the AMIMUN team has done. I extend my warm wishes and support to the entire team for the upcoming AMIMUN 2021 conference.
All the very best!

Mr. Karan Kanodia

Director General, AMIMUN '20

AMIMUN is one of the most prestigious conferences in the MUN circuit. I am extremely grateful to be a part of its legacy.
I have seen it all in AMIMUN be it as a delegate in my first year of college, an OC member in the second year, leading the marketing team in my third year to being the Director General of the conference in its 2020 edition. Behind the deliberation and discussion that goes on inside the committees for 3 days, there is a huge team of students that work day and night for months to make it possible. AMIMUN teaches you life skills, how to handle different critical situations, how to manage people and align them to the common goal which is to have the best conference by far every year.
I know delegates coming in this year will have a great experience as well. As an Advisor to this e-Edition of AMIMUN, I will try to the best of my capacity to help the team learn from our previous mistakes and make new ones for themselves. I wish best of luck to my juniors who are working tirelessly to make the e-Edition of AMIMUN a success, I believe in them.
All the very best!
