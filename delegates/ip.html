International Press

Amity International Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nations spanning over two days. At AMIMUN, delegates gain in-depth knowledge of diplomacy, international relations, and the inner workings of the United Nations.

Committee Introduction

"In a post-truth era, reportage not only defines access to information but also the perception of truth itself." At AMIMUN’21 we believe that the job of International Press is one that requires utmost commitment, sincerity and belief in the ideals of media itself. Reporting during AMIMUN’21 will require a conscientious and diligent approach to provide emphasis on not only the substantive policy changes that occur but how that change translates into the real world. As the International Press Corps, it is one's prerogative to bring out the truth to the masses and make certain there is democratized access to the functioning of the various organs of AMIMUN'21. It is your imperative, in this era of freedom; to that ensure change must be accessible to all.
